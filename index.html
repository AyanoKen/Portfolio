Unity VR - Echos of War

Does anyone ever win in a war? Humanity's history is rich but at the same time stained in blood. So many victories but the foundation is the corpses and woes of millions. Was it ever worth the sufferings?

"Echos of War", is an immersive VR museum experience that takes users through the haunting memories and stories left in the wake of war. Each room and corridor is meticulously crafted with visual storytelling, ambient soundscapes, and meaningful narratives that guide the user through a powerful journey of remembrance and reflection. With detailed environments and an intuitive flow between exhibits, Echoes of War challenges the boundary between virtual and reality, offering a deeply moving experience that emphasizes the importance of memory and history.

Team Size: 1
Development Cycle: 6 weeks
Highlights: Virtual Museum, Games for Change, Dynamic Scene Render
